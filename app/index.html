Our Specialties

Website Maintenance

A Worry Free website!

Every website, over time, will need maintenance. SEO will need to be re-optimized. Content will get old or need to be changed. Pages may need to be deleted or added. Plug-ins need updating or changing. Different things can affect your site.

We can offer you a monthly maintenance package that will take care of all these possibilities, giving you a website that is truly worry free!

Social Media

Make your presence known!

Social Media is a great way to reach out to our your customers. Let them know what you have to offer and get feedback on what inspires them. We can set up all your social media accounts and coordinate them with your website.

Once set up it can take time keeping up with all the social media relevant to your business. We offer a social media package that includes posting to all your sites as often as you want. Posts are written in a professional yet friendly voice. Package can be tailored to fit your needs. Let us talk to your customers when you are too busy.

Your Business Blog

Keep your customers up-to-date!

Social media is important, a blog is vital. A blog allows you to interact with your customers in-depth. You can have blog posts on your companies process, what makes you unique and desirable, and what you might have coming up. Blog posts can also advertise events you might be involved in or limited time specials.

We can set up your blog for you. Once set up we can offer you a package where we will write your blog posts for you. We can do posts as frequently as you require. Blog posts are written in a professional and friendly voice. Let us keep your customers up-to-date on everything exciting going on in your business!
