Our Features

Our Websites

Every website we do is designed with you to capture the feel and look of your business. Colors, fonts, images, the sky is the limit! You can choose from a template or we can custom code your site. Every site is fully responsive, looking perfect no matter what size screen you're using. We can even set up your email accounts and link them with your email provider.

Extras

What if you need more than a standard website? Do you need an e-commerce option? No problem, we can set you up a site, optimize it, even connect it to your Facebook page so you can sell there as well. What about hosting? We can provide you with hosting for your site and can even help you obtain your domain name if you don't have one. What about maintenance, we can set you up with a program to do regular maintenance and SEO updates so your site can be truly trouble free.

Design Branding

We can start bycreating a custom logo for your business. We can take your website design and use it to create backgrounds for your Facebook, Twitter & other social media sites. We can provide you with mock-ups of your design with business cards. This gives your brand a distinct & cohesive look across platforms and makes it instantly recognizable.

Social Media

We all know that in this day and age, having a social media presence can make all the difference. We offer a complete setup package to setup and style social media sites for you. We also offer a program where we will provide regular updates to your site based on a schedule you choose. This program is not only available if we build your site. If you already have a site and are looking to get out from under your social media tasks give us a call. No more blog posts, Facebook updates, tweets! You focus on your business and we'll focus on keeping you relevant on social media.
